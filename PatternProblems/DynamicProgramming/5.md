- This problem deals with 2D dp
- Here we have to decide what to do when matches are found and when they are not found
When match is found we just increment the ans and also move both the pointers
When match is not found we have two options either move the pointer of text1 or move the pointer of text2
We will take the maximum of both the options and store it in our dp array
- Finally we will return the value at the end of our dp array which will be the length of the longest common subsequence
- Time complexity is O(n*m) where n and m are the lengths of the two strings
- Space complexity is also O(n*m) due to the dp array we are using to store the results of subproblems.
